About crème

Strut your style in the new online fashion game, crème. Join other shopping obsessed and aspiring fashionistas in crème’s platform of modern style. Before entering the virtual world of crème, take the ‘crème test’ to ensure you are ready to join the other fashion elite users within crème.

the crème test
The crème test challenges your knowledge of current market prices of various fashion items, from high-end luxury brands to chic-bargain brands. Choose the corresponding market price or range for each fashion item; this test resembles the game show “The Price is Right” —but oh, so much more chic. For further instructions about the test form itself, click "Instructions" above. Answer all the questions in the crème test correctly to ensure you are ready for the world of crème.
You can begin the test by clicking the "crème" button below. Goodluck!
