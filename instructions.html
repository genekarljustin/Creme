Radio Input



Select your answer by clicking on the corresponding circle of your answer.

Checkbox



Select your answer by clicking on the corresponding checkbox of your answer. Multiple checkboxes can be checked at one time, but for this game one only one checkbox should be checked.

List and drop-down list


Select your answer by clicking on the corresponding number for your answer.

Slider

Two pictures will be shown, and you will have to choose between the left or right picture.

Text Input

Type in the numerical value of your answer, only numerical values can be inputted. A dollar sign should not be inputted. Do not type in any spaces when entering your answer.
